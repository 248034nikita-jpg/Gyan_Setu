About Us

Bridging children to knowledge

Gyan Setu means "bridge of knowledge" in Nepali. We are a small team of developers and designers from Nepal building educational tools for the next generation.

Our Mission

Make quality education accessible to every child in Nepal regardless of location or income.

Our Approach

Game-based bilingual learning that respects Nepal's culture and works on low-bandwidth networks.

Privacy First

No email required. No ads. Children's data is never shared. Parents stay in full control.
